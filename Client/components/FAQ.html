6.How do I wash bamboo products?
Bamboo products such as clothing, towels, and bedding can generally be machine washed in cold or warm water on a gentle cycle. Use a mild detergent without bleach or fabric softeners to avoid damaging the bamboo fibers. Avoid using hot water or high-temperature settings, as they can cause shrinkage or damage to the fabric.

7.How do I store bamboo products?
Store bamboo products in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent discoloration or damage. Avoid storing bamboo products in plastic bags or containers for extended periods, as this can trap moisture and lead to mildew or odors.
